# Breznian Defense Forces

## Overview

The Republic of Breznia was the underdog in it's war for independence, nearly 50 years ago. Ever since their independence Breznians have had to fight for their survival.

Large defense investments and procurement over the past several years mean that The Breznian Defense Force has made it's way to being a formadible fighting force in nearly record time.

Being one the original founding members of the Mutual Security Alliance (MSA), Breznia is committed to contributing to global peacekeeping operations.
