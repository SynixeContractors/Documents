# Royal Nyrland Armed Forces

## Overview

The Kingdom of Nyrland is a constitutional monarchy with a rich history in foreign expeditions and maritime trade. It is a rich and technologically adept nation, focussing it's economy highly on trade and information technologies.

It is a small nation with a relatively small military, but punches greatly above it's own weight. It's forces are equipped with contemporary equipment and firearms; they are also very well trained. They are also no strangers to conflict, eagerly contributing forces to international peacekeeping operations.

Being one of the original founding members of the Mutual Security Alliance (MSA), Nyrland is always committed to fulfilling any and all security agreements.
