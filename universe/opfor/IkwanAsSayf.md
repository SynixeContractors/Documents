# Ikwan-As-Sayf

## Overview

Ikwan-As-Sayf, or 'the Brothers of the Sword', is a radical militant organization that operates across remote regions and unstable provinces. They are formed of an operate in splintered cells of various insurgent networks, working through strict ideological zeal and a ruthless paramilitary structure.

Despite not being a formal military force, they are well equipped; utilising a range of modern/contemporate weapons and various surplus equipment.

## Information

Primary Language: Farsi and Arabic
## Military Equipment

Camoflage Pattern: Varied, surplus camouflage and civilian clothing.
Platform: 7.62 (AK), 5.45 (AK), 7.62 (NATO), 5.56 STANAG
