# Ikwan-As-Sayf

## Overview

Ikwan-As-Sayf, or 'the Brothers of the Sword', is a radical militant organization that operates across remote regions and unstable provinces. Driven by a belief that armed struggle is the only path to their vision of a purified homeland, Ikwan as Sayf targets government forces, foreign military presence, and rival factions alike. Formed from splinter cells of various insurgent networks, the group blends strict ideological zeal with a ruthless paramilitary structure. They rely on guerrilla tactics, improvised weapons, and decentralized leadership, making them unpredictable and difficult to eradicate.

Though fragmented, the group maintains cohesion through shared doctrine, charismatic field commanders, and a steady flow of black‑market arms.
