# Sons of the Coming Dawn

## Overview

The Sons of the Coming Dawn is a radical militant organization born from fringe survivalist circles, apocalyptic cult rhetoric, and disillusioned veterans. Operating across rural safehouses and abandoned industrial zones, the group believes that modern society is on the brink of collapse - and that they must accelerate its downfall to "prepare the faithful" for a new era.

Though fragmented into autonomous cells, the Sons maintain unity through shared prophecy, encrypted communications and a steady flow of black-market weapons.
