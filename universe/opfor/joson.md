# Joson Socialist Republic

## Overview

Joson is federalized republic consisting of 5 historically warring nations united by common class struggle in the early XXth century. With significant decentralization and strong self-sufficiency across most of the sectors of economy they have proven themselves to be stable against many economic hardships of the region.

National Poeple's Army inheriting its ideas from the civilian parts of the government is largely self-sufficient producing and using its arms to spread revolution abroad in countries forgotten by the globalized world.
